The Syntactic Structures conference was held in Moscow in 2007 and 2008.

The first Syntactic Structures conference (5-6 April 2007) was devoted to the 50th anniversary of Noam Chomsky's Syntactic Structures but was not limited to any theoretical framework. The program is available here; and here you can find photos taken by Barbara Partee (several of them are used above, at the top of this web page.)
